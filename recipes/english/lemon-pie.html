Lemon Pie

Ingredients

Crust

  • Butter 150 grams
  • Flour 270 grams
  • Sugar 4.2 tablespoons
  • Whole eggs 2 units
  • Egg yolk 1 unit
  • Baking powder 2 teaspoons
  • Salt A pinch

Filling

  • Lemons 4 units
  • Eggs 2 units
  • Cornstarch 2 tablespoons
  • Water 2 cups
  • Butter 32 grams

Meringue

  • Egg white 5 units
  • Sugar 4 tablespoons

Steps

Crust

  1. Mix the butter with the sugar until it forms a paste
  2. Later add the whole eggs with the yolk
  3. With a sifter, sift the rest of the ingredients and add it to the mix
  4. Mix all together until it forms a dough
  5. Once done, place it in a recipient, making sure to make a border with the excess of the dough on the sides of it to contain the filling later
  6. Place it on the oven until the dough is golden

Filling

  1. In a pot on low heat, add all the ingredients and whisk with a wooden spoon until it thickens
  2. Once thickened, place it on top of the crust

Meringue

  1. Whisk with a whisker the whites while you add the sugar until all the sugar is disolved and it stays in place once you remove the whisker
  2. Place it on top of the filling in form of little cones and put it in the oven for 1 or 2 minutes or until the cones golden
